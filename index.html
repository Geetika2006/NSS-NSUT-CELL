About NSS NSUT

The NSS unit at Netaji Subhas University of Technology aims to instill the spirit of voluntary work and national service among students. Through various activities like blood donation camps, cleanliness drives, and awareness campaigns, NSS NSUT plays a vital role in community development.

Our Initiatives

Blood Donation Camps

Organized across campus, saving hundreds of lives every year.

Cleanliness Drives

Promoting a cleaner, greener campus and city under Swachh Bharat.

Awareness Campaigns

Workshops on mental health, gender sensitization, and civic duties.
